About Specialty Barista Training

Specialty Barista Training is dedicated to empowering coffee lovers and professionals with the knowledge and skills to excel in the world of specialty coffee. Our mission is to make high-quality barista education accessible, practical, and engaging for everyone—whether you’re just starting out or looking to refine your craft.

Our Mission

We believe that great coffee starts with great training. Our platform offers free, interactive lessons, expert guides, and hands-on activities to help you master every aspect of coffee preparation, service, and appreciation.

Meet the Team

Our team brings years of experience from top cafés, competitions, and coffee education programs. We’re passionate about sharing our knowledge and supporting your journey in specialty coffee.
