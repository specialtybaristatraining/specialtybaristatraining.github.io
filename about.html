About Specialty Barista Training

Specialty Barista Training is your gateway to mastering the art and science of coffee. We empower enthusiasts and professionals alike with expert-led lessons, hands-on guides, and immersive learning experiences tailored to the specialty coffee industry.

Our Mission

We believe that exceptional coffee begins with exceptional training. Our platform is designed to be inclusive, practical, and inspiring—offering free resources that cover everything from espresso fundamentals to advanced brewing techniques.

Meet the Team

Our team includes award-winning baristas, certified trainers, and coffee educators from renowned cafés and competitions. We're united by a passion for coffee and a commitment to helping you grow your skills and confidence behind the bar.
