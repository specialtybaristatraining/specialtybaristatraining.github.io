About Specialty Barista Training

Specialty Barista Training is your gateway to mastering the art and science of coffee. We empower enthusiasts and professionals alike with expert-led lessons, hands-on guides, and immersive learning experiences tailored to the specialty coffee industry.

Our Mission

We believe that exceptional coffee begins with exceptional training. Our platform is designed to be inclusive, practical, and inspiring—offering free resources that cover everything from espresso fundamentals to advanced brewing techniques.

Meet the Team

Our team includes award-winning baristas, certified trainers, and coffee educators from renowned cafés and competitions. We're united by a passion for coffee and a commitment to helping you grow your skills and confidence behind the bar.

Sukram Lama — Lead Trainer

Seasoned specialty barista and trainer bringing international café standards to Nepal.

Sukram Lama is a specialty barista and coffee educator with more than a decade of professional experience across Malaysia, the UAE, and Nepal. He began at The Coffee Bean & Tea Leaf in Malaysia (2013–2015), where in 2014 he was selected as a Top‑Ten finalist in the company’s barista competition. Sukram then advanced to Costa Coffee in Dubai (2016–2020) before joining the UAE specialty scene at Press Specialty Coffee in Dibba Al Fujairah (2021) and The View by Wave in Khor Fakkan (2022–2023).

In 2023 Sukram returned to Nepal and founded several local ventures that combine hospitality with practical training:

  • Taboche Restaurant — a fusion dining experience in Siddhapokhari, Bhaktapur
  • Specialty Barista Training Center — structured courses and hands‑on training to raise local barista standards
  • Royal Java Time Coffee Shop — a neighborhood café focused on quality and warmth

He trains aspiring baristas in espresso technique, milk preparation, latte art, and café operations—combining technical precision with practical drills and warm hospitality to help trainees meet international standards.
